I created a simple game about a little sentient flame who is stuck in the middle of a rainstorm. The flame must avoid the falling raindrops to survive!
Using the arrow keys, the player can move the flame across the screen. The user can press the “F” key to shoot a fireball upwards and destroy one raindrop per fireball. The user can only shoot three fireballs at a time, each fireball having a three second cooldown. The flame has three lives, and once it gets hit by the rain three times, the flame dies.
The goal is to survive in the rainstorm (and gain the most points) for as long as possible.

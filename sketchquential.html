Collaborating with a group of students, we crafted a collaborative drawing card game.
Work with the collective to draw randomly selected prompts before time runs out!
User Manual:
Setup:
- Split cards into respective colors and categories
- Shuffle the cards well
- Form a circle facing outwards
What to Draw?
- Pull one prompt card of each color (blue: adjective, yellow: noun, red: verb/action)
- Assemble into full prompt, Madlib-style: blue, yellow, red
- The player who pulled the prompt pulls a piece of drawing paper, marking it with one tally in the corner
Let’s Start!
- They then pass the marked piece of drawing paper to the player to their right and start counting:
- everyone not drawing counts to (approximately):
- Easy: 20 sec
- Intermediate: 10 sec
- Hard: 5 sec
- Once the countdown is over, the drawing is passed clockwise
- Each cycle, the person who pulled the prompt must quickly mark the paper with another tally before passing it on. Game is complete after five cycles

My art professor challenged my classmates and I to create a self portrait using objects that hold meaning to us. While doubling as a value study, I decided to create a composition of my various notebooks, all containers of my random spurts of inspiration, brainstorming, task tracking, and the like. While my notebooks are a scribbled mess, they keep me organized and attentive.
(14x17", charcoal on paper)
